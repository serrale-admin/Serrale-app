# Client Home Guideline

## Target feel
- Simple
- Warm
- Premium
- Fast
- Trustworthy
- Ethiopian
- Action-oriented

## Mandatory copy (current baseline)
- `Hello, Nati`
- `Find trusted experts for your next project`
- `Search services or experts...`
- `Talents. Skills. Community.`
- `Ethiopia's talent, working together.`
- `Post a Project`

## Required home order
1. Header (logo, notifications, avatar)
2. Greeting block
3. Search bar + filter
4. Community hero banner with primary CTA
5. Popular Categories (4 cards + View More)
6. Recommended Providers (3 cards + View More)
7. Your Projects (recent project card + View All)
8. Post-project CTA card
9. Bottom navigation

## Role separation
- Client app must not show provider dashboard concepts.
- Exclude from client home:
  - Jobs feed
  - Proposal tracking KPIs
  - Earnings and availability panels

## Component targets
- `ClientHomeHeader.tsx`
- `SearchFilterBar.tsx`
- `CommunityHeroBanner.tsx`
- `ClientCategoryCard.tsx`
- `RecommendedProviderCard.tsx`
- `ClientProjectCard.tsx`
- `PostProjectCTA.tsx`
- `ClientBottomTabBar.tsx`
